About Serry

I am Abdul Serry Koroma, known as Africanist Serry, a passionate youth leader from Sierra Leone who speaks for unity, education, and pride in African heritage.

Through messages, videos, and community outreach, I inspire the next generation to rise and build the Africa they dream of.

I believe the future of Africa begins with our voices, our children, and our purpose.
